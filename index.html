The Problem

Our inspirations for this project were our personal experiences with physical therapy, and the struggle that patients endure during their treatment. Patients often feel unmotivated and gain a sense of disengagement, leading to an unsuccessful session of physical therapy.

0 Over 50 percent of all Americans over the age of 18 develop an injury that could benefit from physical therapy.
0 Around 20% of physical therapy patients drop out of their treatment within the first 3 visits.
0 70% of physical therapy patients fail to complete their full course of care.
Our Features

Within our Web & Mobile apps

The following features make physical treatment more engaging for both the patient and therapist.

Recordings

Use our IOS App to record your workout or stretch sessions. Our technology will count your reps for you, give you instant feedback, find your average heart rate, and time your session.

Communication

Communicate with your therapist more than ever! Therapists are able to set various exercises and schedules for their patients and assess their patients' exercise over time.

Reminders

Therapists are able to set which exercises to add for different patients, which will then be enforced to the patient. With our prominent UI, patients are prompted to do their exercises properly.

Statistics

Our apps are connected to a database that allows therapists to take a look at past sessions. Our Web App has graphs and statistics for each video for the therapist to analyze the patient's progress.
